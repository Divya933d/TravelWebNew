Discover London

The vibrant capital of England with history, royalty, and charm.

Best Time to Visit

Visit during spring (March to May) or autumn (September to November) for pleasant weather, fewer crowds, and blooming gardens.

Environment & Locality

London is a bustling metropolis with a mix of old-world charm and modern marvels. From the River Thames to royal landmarks, the city's environment is rich in culture and green spaces. Locals are diverse, polite, and fast-paced.

Famous Foods

Try classic fish and chips, full English breakfast, and “Sunday Roast.” Don’t miss sweet treats like scones with clotted cream and Victoria sponge cake.
